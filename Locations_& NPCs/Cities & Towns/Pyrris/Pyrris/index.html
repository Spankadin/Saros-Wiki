    Pyrris

    The majestic capital of Saros, Pyrris perches atop a colossal cliffside, overlooking the sprawling expanse of the kingdom. Home to the illustrious Radiant Citadel, the city pulses with life, and its streets teem with a large traveler contingency seeking counsel and aid from the Queen. The only way to Pyrris is the High Road from Goslow.

    Population - 500
