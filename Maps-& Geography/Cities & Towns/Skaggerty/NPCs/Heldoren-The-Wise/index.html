    NPC Sage

    Heldoren The Wise

    The high priest who oversees the Temple of Austerus in Skaggerty. An elderly, withered man, short and bent with age, barely able to support his own weight with his cane. Besides carrying out general priestly duties, he also takes on the role of sage due to the low population of the town.
