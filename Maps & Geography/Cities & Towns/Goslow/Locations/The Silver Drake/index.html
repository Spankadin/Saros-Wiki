    The Silver Drake

    Overseen by Lone-Wolf Louie, The Silver Drake is one of the most famous Gambler’s Guild halls in all of Saros, 2nd only to the massive hall in Rore. The Silver Drake is a tightly ran and well secured building where the foolhardy come from all over Saros to test their luck and their coin with hopes of leaving Goslow as rich as Lord Goldhaven himself!
