Wonderful foods, lavish entertainment, and games of all sorts are plentiful during [[The_Spring_Harvest_Festival|The Festival]]. The Spring Harvest is celebrated by most civilized peoples of [[../A_Brief_Saros_History|Saros]] in their own ways, but the largest celebration is held annually at the Capitol in [[../../Locations_& NPCs/Cities & Towns/Pyrris/Pyrris|Pyrris]]. 

A long standing tradition, predating even [[../../Locations_& NPCs/Cities & Towns/Pyrris/NPCs/Queen_Amara|Queen Amara]]'s rule, it's a busy time filled with anticipation, as ripened fruits are being collected and new seeds are planted with hopes of a bountiful fall harvest. Spring rains nourish the young plants, and farmers must be vigilant in protecting their crops from pests and disease. While also inventorying and delivering their goods to the Festival.
