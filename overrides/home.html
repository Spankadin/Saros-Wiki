Welcome to Saros

Near four thousand and two hundred years ago, the wizard Caelum made a desperate wish to save what remained of his people from the consequences of his greatest pupil’s choice.

That wish carried them away from Mystara to an uncharted continent — a place of beauty, peril, fey-line power, forgotten delves, and scattered opportunity. Over time, Saros became a land where dwarven ruins, elven magic, frontier towns, and dangerous ambition all meet.

It is a setting built for discovery: old roads, hidden strongholds, rising settlements, faction conflict, strange magic, and stories waiting to be pulled out of the dark.
