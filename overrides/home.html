Welcome to Saros

Near four thousand and two hundred years ago, the wizard Caelum made a desperate wish to save what remained of his people from the consequences of his greatest pupil’s choice. That wish carried them away from Mystara to an uncharted continent — a place of beauty, peril, fey-line power, forgotten delves, and scattered opportunity.

Over time, Saros became a land where dwarven ruins, elven magic, dangerous frontiers, and rising settlements all overlap. Ancient delves lie hidden beneath the earth, many of them once shaped by the cooperation of dwarves and elves who learned to harness the magical currents known as fey-lines. Some of those sites are now lost, others are known only in rumor, and a few have grown into the foundations of towns, trade routes, and conflict.

Across the continent, major cities and isolated communities alike are shaped by old legends, faction ambition, merchant interests, political pressure, and the constant pull of the unknown. From ports and frontier towns to mountain strongholds, sacred places, barrows, landmarks, and delves, Saros is a setting built for discovery.

This wiki is meant to serve as a living record of that world — its history, locations, NPCs, clans, factions, rules, and mysteries. Whether you are looking for a town, a legend, a person of influence, a point of lore, or a path into the campaign itself, Saros is meant to feel like a world with depth beneath every road and story around every fire.
